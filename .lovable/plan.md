

# A1 Engineering - Professional Multi-Page Landing Website

## Project Overview
A high-conversion, SEO-optimized multi-page website for A1 Engineering, featuring a sophisticated industrial design aesthetic with glassmorphism effects, metallic gradients, and professional imagery that conveys engineering expertise and trustworthiness.

---

## Visual Design System

### Color Palette (Derived from Logo)
- **Primary**: Industrial Metallic Blue (#3B5F7B)
- **Secondary**: Deep Navy Slate (#1E2A3A)
- **Accent**: Steel Silver (#C0C8D1)
- **Background**: Blueprint White (#F5F7FA)
- **Gradients**: Subtle metallic blue-to-navy transitions

### Typography
- **Headings**: Montserrat Bold (matches "A1" lettering)
- **Body**: Inter (clean, professional readability)

### Design Effects
- Glassmorphism cards with blur and transparency
- Metallic gradient overlays
- Blueprint-style decorative elements
- High-quality stock imagery of engineering work

---

## Site Structure (5 Pages)

### 1. Home Page (Landing)
**Hero Section**
- Full-screen hero with blueprint/construction background
- Headline: "Building the Future of Land Development: From Survey to Final Permit"
- Sub-headline about PE-led expertise and comprehensive services
- Dual CTAs: "Start Your Project" (primary) + "View Our Services" (secondary)
- Trust badges: Licensed PE states, US-Based, Certified Compliance

**Process Section**
- Sleek 4-step numbered workflow with animated reveals:
  1. Analysis & Survey (GeoTechnical, Site Analysis)
  2. Drafting & Design (Architectural, Site Plans, Floor Plans)
  3. Engineering & Compliance (Load Calcs, Green Code, Foundation)
  4. Approval & Permits (Upload, Permits, Certificate of Occupancy)

**Quick Service Overview**
- 5 category cards linking to Services page
- Visual icons for each category

**Trust Bar**
- "Licensed PE Approval" | "Certified Green/Energy Compliance" | "US-Based Expertise"

### 2. Services Page
**Service Matrix with Expandable Accordions**
- **Planning & Design**: Site Plans, Floor Plans, Elevations, Renders, Architectural Drawings, Zoning Consultations
- **Engineering & Structural**: Foundation Design, GeoTechnical Survey, Engineering Inspections, Special Inspections
- **Green, Energy & Testing**: Blower Door Test, Duct Test, HERS Index, Green Code Sign-off
- **PE Electrical Engineering**: Load Calculations, Panel Schedules, Solar PV, EV Charging, Generator/Backup, HVAC Electrical, Multi-family Design, Lighting & Security
- **Permitting & Operations**: Electronic Upload, Building Permits, Certificate of Occupancy, Fence/Demo Permits, Printing & Notary

### 3. About Page
**Founders Story**
- Narrative about Mechanical + Electrical Engineer founders who met in college
- "Decades of Field Experience" + "Rigorous Academic Roots" messaging
- Professional founder photos (placeholder)

**The PE-Led Advantage**
- Prominent display of "US-Based and Led by Licensed Professional Engineers"
- Explanation of what this means for clients

**State Licensing Map**
- Interactive or visual map showing PE certification coverage
- Clear badge display: TN, TX, AZ, FL with electrical sign-off capability

### 4. Contact / Get a Quote Page
**Project Inquiry Form**
- Name (required)
- Phone (required)
- Email (required)
- Project Address
- Property Type (Dropdown: Residential, Commercial, Multi-family)
- Services Needed (Multi-select checkboxes)
- Project Description (textarea)
- Form ready for Resend integration later

**Contact Information**
- Phone, email, business hours
- US-Based location emphasis

### 5. FAQ Page (Optional/Future)
- Common questions about permitting, timelines, PE services
